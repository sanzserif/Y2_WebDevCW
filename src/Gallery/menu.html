     Piano Legends     
Art Tatum
Tatum was a virtuoso pianist known for his incredible technique, speed, and improvisation skills. He was a major influence on other pianists, including Oscar Peterson and Bill Evans.
Glenn Gould
Gould was a Canadian pianist known for his interpretations of the works of Johann Sebastian Bach. He was known for his precise, almost mechanical playing style, and for his unconventional interpretations of classical music.
Herbie Hancock
AHancock is a jazz pianist and composer known for his fusion of jazz, funk, and rock styles. He has won numerous Grammy Awards and has collaborated with many other musicians throughout his career.
Sergei Rachmaninoff
Rachmaninoff was a Russian composer and pianist known for his Romantic-era music, including his Concerto No. 2 in C Minor. He was also known for his large hands, which allowed him to play complex chords and octaves.
Sun Ra
Sun Ra was a jazz pianist and composer known for his avant-garde music and his unique approach to performance, which often included elaborate costumes and theatrical elements. He was a major figure in the free jazz movement.

     Famous Orchestras     
The Young People's Chorus of New York City
The Young People's Chorus of New York City is a youth choir that was founded in 1988. They have performed at a variety of prestigious venues, including Carnegie Hall and the Kennedy Center. They are known for their innovative approach to choral music, incorporating elements of pop, world music, and contemporary classical music into their repertoire.
The London Symphony Orchestra
The London Symphony Orchestra is one of the world's most renowned orchestras. They have performed at a wide range of venues, including the Barbican Centre in London and Lincoln Center in New York. They are known for their versatility and their ability to perform a wide range of classical and contemporary music.
The Kronos Quartet
The Kronos Quartet is a contemporary string quartet founded in 1973. They are known for their innovative approach to classical music, incorporating elements of world music, jazz, and rock into their repertoire. They have collaborated with a wide range of artists, from Philip Glass to Bollywood composer A.R. Rahman.
